My Work

Watch me evolve creatively over the course of several years. I stated writing serisouly in the eighth grade when I decided I wanted to go to a school for the arts in my hommetown. Ever since then, I've been writing non-stop and have had my work published in local youth anthologies and blogs, and have read my work at numerous events.

I got my first DSLR camera for Christmas when I was 15 years old and started offering mini photoshoots to my friends and family. Eventually word got around and people started approaching me to shoot birthdays, concerts, events, and headshots.
